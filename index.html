Eline Visser

I'm a researcher at Uppsala University funded by the Wenner-Gren Foundations. I'm an expert on Kalamang (Papuan) and Uruangnirin (Austronesian), the languages spoken on the Karas Islands in eastern Indonesia. I have also collected data on other Austronesian languages of eastern Indonesia: Geser-Gorom, Onim and Yamdena. Until mid 2026, I'm leading a project called Mapping Bomberai, which aims to collect data on the understudied languages of that peninsula (see below).

I collaborate with researchers from among others Google to understand how well large language models can read grammars and process field recordings. See this paper about grammar-reading (follow-up in this tweet) and this unpublished paper about grammar-reading and audio processing.

I have a side-project on Dutch multi-ethnolect as it is used in hip hop lyrics. A database with 6695 lyrics is available here.

Previously, I worked on Scandinavian prosody in the ExSynOp project at UiT The Arctic University of Norway.

My PhD is from Lund University (Sweden), for which I wrote a grammar of Kalamang, a Papuan language of eastern Indonesia and a neighbour language of Uruangnirin. I also made a dictionary and an archive.

Mapping Bomberai (2024–2026)

In this project I aim to collect data on the understudied languages of the Bomberai peninsula. It is financed by the Wenner-Gren Foundations with administrative support from Uppsala University and the Center for Endangered Languages Documentation in Manokwari, Indonesia.

Publications directed at the Kalamang and Uruangnirin communities

2023. Eline Visser. Kamus bahasa Wangniring. [Wangniring dictionary.] A free mobile phone app with 1800 Uruangnirin lemmas and Papuan Malay translations, some example sentences, and 360 pictures. Made with SIL's Dictionary App Builder.

2021. Eline Visser. Kamus bahasa Kalamang. [Kalamang dictionary.] A free mobile phone app with 3800 Kalamang lemmas, Indonesian and English translations, example sentences, and more than 600 pictures. Made with SIL's Dictionary App Builder.

2019. Eline Visser, Fajaria Yarkuran and Kamaruddin Gusek. Kuawi. Dongeng bahasa Kalamang. [Kuawi. A Kalamang tale.] A children's book based on a Kalamang oral narrative, illustrated by Kalamang school children. Distributed in Mas, Karas, Indonesia. Self-published.
